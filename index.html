About

I am an incoming MAE student at the University of Chicago. Before joining UChicago, I spent two years at the European Central Bank, working on macroprudential policy, financial stability, stress testing, and forecasting tools.

My interests lie at the intersection of macro-finance, banking, financial stability, risk modelling, and applied econometrics. I am particularly interested in empirical and computational methods for studying financial institutions, systemic risk, and economic policy.
